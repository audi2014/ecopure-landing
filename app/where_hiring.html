About

At EcoPure Home Cleaning of Hoboken, we understand that every home is unique, so we approach each potential client with meticulous attention to their particular needs. We leveraged our extensive experience to develop an effective model built on these core principles: People, Process, and Quality Control.

Living in Hoboken, you’re likely a busy professional with no time and desire to clean your own home. That is why finding a reliable and trustworthy maid service is essential to a more fulfilled living. Did you know that numerous studies suggest that a cleaner, healthier home environment has a direct impact on your health and overall quality of life? At last, Hoboken can now take advantage of EcoPure’s proven model that is second to none in providing our residents’ peace of mind and satisfaction that the homes are pampered the way they expect. Taking great pride in revolutionizing the residential cleaning concept by incorporating state-of-the-art technology with a process cultivated largely by customer feedback, we set new industry standards for excellence in quality and customer service.

We Love Babies and Pets

Kids and pets fill our lives with meaning and joy. We understand how sensitive our clients can be to the needs of their loved ones. We will work with you tirelessly to make sure your kids and pets are as comfortable as possible. For example, we can be flexible to schedule your service not to disturb your kids’ knapp time or around the time your dog is being walked. Most of the times, however, dogs, cats and other animals are very comfortable and safe around us and the equipment we use.

Our Unique Cleaning Methodology

Unlike other cleaners, we use state-of-the-art technology and wash surfaces where safe & possible, instead of wiping them.

Rainbow is our state-of-the art machine that uses water instead of bags and filters to trap dust, hair, debris, etc. To Rainbow means to use applicable attachments to eliminate dust, debris, hair, crumbs, and dandruff from every surface of your home.
