FAQ's

Will I be locked into a contract?

No, you can cancel your recurring cleanings at any time. Just make sure you do so 24 hours prior to your next scheduled appointment to stop your next service. It’s that simple.

Can I skip or reschedule an appointment?

Absolutely. Just make sure you do so one day before your scheduled appointment. We will then work with you to resume your scheduled seamless periodic cleaning experience.

Can I modify my service ad-hoc or make special requests?

Yes, just call or email your local office with your request and we will be happy to accommodate.
